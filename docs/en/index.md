# Why are we building an Open Source Smart Home?

Welcome to our **SmartHome** project!

This blog is an open story of building our home ecosystem. We are building a family assistant that is:
1. **Private**: All data stays within our perimeter.
2. **Open**: We share our code and architecture.
3. **Family-oriented**: Designed for the convenience of every family member.

We believe technology should serve people, not the other way around. Here you will find descriptions of our mistakes, successes, and technical details on how we automated our home.

Welcome to our journey!
