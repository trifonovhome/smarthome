# Our Hardware: Hardware Inventory

To build a reliable smart home, you need a stable foundation. Here is the hardware our project runs on:

*   **OpenClaw Host (esh.local)**: GEEKOM IT13S Mini PC (Intel i9-13900HK, 32GB RAM). This is the "brain" of our assistant, processing all requests, managing logic, and storing backups.
*   **Home Assistant (homeassistant.local)**: NiPoGi Essenx E1 (AMD Ryzen 3250U, 8GB RAM). Handles communication with physical devices in the home.
*   **Family Desktop**: iMac (24", M1). The main computer for the family.
*   **Router**: Keenetic. Our central network traffic manager.

We selected these devices based on the principle of low power consumption + high performance + reliability.
